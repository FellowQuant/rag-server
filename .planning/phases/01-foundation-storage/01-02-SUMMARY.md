## Decisions Made
- `expire_on_commit=False` on `async_sessionmaker` — avoids `MissingGreenlet` errors when accessing ORM attributes after commit in async context
- FK pragma registered via `event.listens_for(engine.sync_engine, "connect")` — SQLite disables FK enforcement by default; listener fires per connection
- `render_as_batch=True` in both offline and online migration contexts — required for SQLite to support `ALTER TABLE` in future migrations
- Hand-written migration (not `alembic revision --autogenerate`) — exact schema control ensures CASCADE, UNIQUE, and indexes match research spec precisely
